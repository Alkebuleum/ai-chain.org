The Protocol

Not just another chain.

AIChain is carved from a single conviction: identity is the primitive everything else depends on. Before transactions, before storage, before intelligence — there must be verified existence. AIChain makes that possible for both biological and artificial minds.


Built on the Alkebuleum ecosystem, AIChain is the dedicated identity and storage layer — lean, purposeful, and built from genesis for the era of autonomous AI agents acting in the world.
